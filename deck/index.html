Feature 02 · Core

Four zones. One swipe.

The body splits into Head · Upper · Lower · Feet. Swipe each zone to switch looks instantly — and steer any zone with quick tags or a free-text request.

Head

Short, long, perm, bangs, center part…

Upper

Shirt, knit, jacket, hoodie, oversized…

Lower

Slacks, denim, wide, skirt, tapered…

Feet

Sneakers, leather, loafers, boots…

Per-zone tuning: 10 guidance tags + a description box regenerate just that zone — the rest stays put.

Feature 03

Try it on — for real

One tap composites the outfit onto your full-body photo, preserving your face and identity. Engineered to stay fast and cheap.
